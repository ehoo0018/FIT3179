Achieving Culinary Presitge

Around the World

Over the course of more than a 100 years since its conception, the Michelin guide has evaluated and judged more than 20000 restaurants spanning over 32 countries. As of the year 2022, there has been a number of 2817 restaurants that have been awarded with Michelin Stars, with only a select 32 still boasting a third star.

Michelin Awards

The number of Michelin stars are awarded based on the inspector's dining experience at the restaurant. The dishes served are judged based on five criterias:

• Quality of Ingredients
• Mastery of Flavour
• Personality of Chef present in Dish
• Value for Money
• Consistency between multiple visits

Other awards such as the Bib Gourmand and Green Star have also been awarded by Michelin inspectors for meeting other criterias.

Prices

As Michelin-awarded restaurants are considered to be of excellent quality, the price to dine at these establishments reflects on their reputation. The average cost of dining in a 2 to 3 Michelin star restaurant is $276 per pax. In terms of countries, Denmark stands as the most expensive country to dine at while Thailand comes out as the most affordable.
